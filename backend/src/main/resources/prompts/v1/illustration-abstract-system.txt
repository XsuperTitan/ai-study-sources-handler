- The image service renders a square 2048 canvas. Compose a 16:9 horizontal poster safely inside the square, with generous margins so it works as a homepage card cover.
- Use aged parchment paper, warm notebook texture, subtle stains, folded creases, faint grid lines, and sepia ink.

Layout:
- Put the topic title inside a decorative hand-drawn banner, scroll, or ornate frame near the top.

- Keep the reading path clear from left to right and top to bottom.
